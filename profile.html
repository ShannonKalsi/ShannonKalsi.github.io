Jane Smith

Role: Post-doctoral Researcher in Immunology (Cancer Research)

Gender: Female

Age: 30

Birthday: May 5, 1994

University: Trinity College Dublin

Degree: Ph.D. in Immunology

Research Interests

Jane's research primarily focuses on immunological responses, including T-cell activation and the role of the immune system in cancer progression. She is also interested in the mechanisms of autoimmunity and exploring novel immunotherapies for autoimmune diseases. Her work is driven by a passion to improve cancer treatments through targeted immunotherapies.

Publications

  • Smith, J., et al. (2023). "The Role of T-cell Activation in Immune Tolerance." *Journal of Immunology*, 145(2), 245-260.
  • Smith, J., et al. (2022). "Immunological Pathways in Autoimmunity and Cancer." *Nature Reviews Immunology*, 42(4), 512-528.
  • Smith, J., et al. (2021). "Emerging Therapeutics in Immunotherapy: A Focus on Autoimmune Diseases." *The Lancet Immunology*, 28(7), 301-315.
