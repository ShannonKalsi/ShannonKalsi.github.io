Key Considerations

  • Typical time points for detecting target knockdown with lipid-mediated siRNA or microRNA mimic delivery are 24-48 hours for mRNA and 48-96 hours for protein.
  • Follow manufacturer’s recommended final concentration. Typically, we use a 10 nM final concentration of siRNA (can be 10-50nM). Use matching concentration of negative control siRNA (scramble siRNA). For example, if the stock concentration is 50µM and you want a final concentration of 25nM, it’s a 1/2000 dilution in your final transfection.
  • Use low-passage cells (<50 passages); ensure cells are healthy and greater than 90% viable before transfection.

Day 0: Preparation

One day before transfection, plate cells in a 6-well plate. Trypsinize cells and resuspend the pellet into 3ml of media without antibiotics. Count cells using a hemocytometer. Seed the appropriate number of cells into a 6-well plate (typically, cells should be 30-50% confluent at the time of transfection). Leave overnight in the incubator. The cell density should be around 30-50% confluent at transfection time.

Day 1: Transfection

On the day of transfection, check the cell confluence and prepare the siRNA mastermix:

Reagents Needed

  • Opti-MEM (Low serum transfection media)
  • Lipofectamine 2000 (Transfecting agent)
  • Scramble siRNA (Negative control, stock conc 50µM)
  • siRNA (stock conc 50µM or lower)

Mastermix Preparation

  • 1 µL of siRNA or scramble + 199 µL of Opti-MEM
  • 5 µL of Lipofectamine 2000 + 195 µL of Opti-MEM

Scale up as necessary based on the number of wells needed.

Transfection Procedure

  1. Prepare 3 x 1.5mL Eppendorfs. In the first, pipette 3µL of siNT + 597µL of Opti-MEM. In the second tube, pipette 15µL of Lipofectamine + 585µL of Opti-MEM. In the third tube, pipette 3µL of siRNA + 597µL of Opti-MEM. In the fourth tube, pipette 15µL of Lipofectamine + 585µL of Opti-MEM. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. After 5 minutes, mix contents of tube 1 and 2 (siNT or scramble mix), and tube 3 and 4 (siRNA mix). Incubate for 10-20 minutes in the hood.
  3. Remove the old media from the 6-well plate and add 1.6 mL of Opti-MEM to each well. Add 400 µL of your mix to the corresponding wells. Mix well.
  4. Incubate cells for 24-48 hours at 37°C in a CO2 incubator until ready for assay.

For RNA assays, 24 hours is usually sufficient. For protein assays, leave cells for 48 hours. Change to normal media after 24 hours if possible.
