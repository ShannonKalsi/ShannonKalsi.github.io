siRNA Transfection Protocol

Steps for siRNA Transfection

Follow these steps for siRNA transfection:

  1. Prepare a 1 μM solution of siRNA in a transfection buffer according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
  2. Seed cells in a 6-well plate, aiming for 70-80% confluency on the day of transfection.
  3. Prepare transfection complex by adding the appropriate volume of transfection reagent to the siRNA solution.
  4. Incubate the complex at room temperature for 15-20 minutes to allow formation of transfection particles.
  5. Carefully add the transfection complex to the cells and gently mix by swirling the plate.
  6. Incubate the cells for 4-6 hours at 37°C in a CO2 incubator.
  7. After incubation, replace the transfection medium with fresh growth medium and incubate overnight.
  8. Evaluate transfection efficiency 24-48 hours post-transfection using qPCR or Western blotting.
